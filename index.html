TOMATO SPAGHETTI

  1. heat enough water for boiling
  2. add one tablespoon salt and mix good boil
  3. one pack spaghetti pasta
  4. press it slightly and stir it a bit
  5. let it boil for nine to ten mins
  6. nicely boiled , strain , spread and keep asili>
  7. boil for three to four mins
  8. skin coming out and strain and remove skin add to the blender
  9. Now, in a wok take two tablespoon oil
  10. one tablespoon chopped garlic and saute
  11. one onion chopped , cook for two to three mins , until soft
  12. Add some capsicum , saute and tomato puree mix and cook
  13. one tablespoon salt , one half red chilly powder , mix and cover with lid
  14. cook on low flame for three to four mins
  15. Remove lid , nicely reduced
  16. one tablespoom suger , one half black pepper powder , one tablespoon mixed harbs
  17. one tablespoon red chilly flakes
  18. Add the boiled spaghetti,fold and mix until well coated
  19. one tablespoon tomato sauce , some cheese
  20. mix again , nicely coated , looks templing and read to serve - HOT TO HOT
